## Outreach Strategy

### Who to Target (Priority Order):

**Tier 1 - Warm Leads (50% response rate)**
- People who engage with your LinkedIn post
- Referrals from friends/network
- Companies posting AI job openings
- Founders who replied to your comments

**Tier 2 - Warm-ish (20% response rate)**
- Companies using tech you specialize in (LangChain, Streamlit, etc.)
- Startups that recently announced AI features
- Companies with "AI" or "ML" in job postings
- SaaS companies with 10-50 employees (sweet spot)

**Tier 3 - Cold (5-10% response rate)**
- LinkedIn search: "founder" + "AI" + "startup"
- AngelList companies with AI in description
- ProductHunt launches mentioning AI
- YC companies (current batch)

**Monday-Friday (30 min/day):**
- 5 personalized LinkedIn DMs (warm leads)

## Qualifying Questions (During First Call)

Ask these to avoid time-wasters:

1. **"What's your timeline for this project?"**
   - Good: "We want to ship by March"
   - Bad: "We're just exploring"

2. **"What's your budget range?"**
   - Good: "$10-20K for MVP"
   - Bad: "We're not sure yet"

3. **"Who makes the final decision?"**
   - Good: "I do" or "Me and my co-founder"
   - Bad: "I need to check with 5 people"

4. **"What happens if we don't build this?"**
   - Good: "We lose customers to competitors"
   - Bad: "Nothing, it's a nice-to-have"
