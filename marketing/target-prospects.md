# Target Prospect List - 20 Companies/Contacts

## How to Use This List

1. Research each company/person (5-10 min)
2. Personalize outreach template with specific observations
3. Send DM/email
4. Log contact in spreadsheet (date, response, next steps)
5. Follow up after 5 days if no response

## Tier 5: Agencies/Consultancies (Partnership Opportunities)

### Partner with Agencies Who Need AI Expertise

These agencies have clients but lack AI engineering:

**Examples:**
- **Fathom & Draft** - Digital product agency
- **Postlight** - Strategy, design, engineering
- **Your Local Tech Agency** - Search "[your city] web development agency"

**Approach:**
"Hi [Agency], I'm an AI/ML engineer specializing in RAG/agents. I work with agencies as a technical partner when their clients need AI features. Would a quick intro call make sense?"

**Why this works:**
- Agencies have deal flow but not AI talent
- You become their "AI expert on retainer"
- Steady pipeline of projects
