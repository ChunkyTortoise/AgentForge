   - Email signature
   - All outreach templates (replace [Calendly Link])

### 2. Post LinkedIn Announcement (15 minutes)
**Why:** This is your launch. Gets you visibility and warm leads.

**How:**
1. Open linkedin-post.md
2. Copy **Version 3** (Problem/Solution format - best for consulting)
3. Customize:
   - Add your location if relevant
   - Add specific tech you love working with
   - Link to your AgentForge GitHub

5. Pin it to your profile

**After posting:**

### 3. Create Tracking Spreadsheet (10 minutes)
**Why:** You'll forget who you contacted and when. This keeps you organized.

**How:**
1. Open Google Sheets

3. Add first 5 prospects from target-prospects.md

### 4. Send Your First 3 Messages (30 minutes)

3. Send via LinkedIn DM (fastest)
